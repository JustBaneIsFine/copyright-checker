## Version 1.3.0

New
- Drag and drop. Drop tracks or whole folders straight onto the window and they all get combined into one batch. Drop several at once or add them one at a time, and remove any you didn't mean to include.
- Support for AIFF audio files.
- The app now checks for updates on its own and shows you what's new, with a one-click download.

Fixes
- Fixed the app sometimes opening to a blank window on macOS.
